How It Works

For Businesses
  1. Register as an admin
  2. Create your company profile
  3. Set up cashiers/service points
  4. Share your unique QR code with customers
  5. Manage your queue in real-time
For Customers
  1. Scan QR code or enter company code
  2. Receive your position and OTP
  3. Wait for your turn, see estimated wait time
  4. Get notified when it's your turn
  5. Proceed to your assigned cashier
Benefits
  • Reduce physical queues
  • Improve customer satisfaction
  • Optimize service efficiency
  • Real-time queue management
